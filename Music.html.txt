    <h1 style= "text-align:center"><b><u>MUSIC</u></b></h1>
    
     <p style="color: rgb(58, 51, 153);"><b>Music is the language of the heart. Remember when you dreamt of being a musician, but those thoughts vanished because you didn't know how to do that?<br>
     Here, we provide you some of the best tips for your music career right after your 12th class.</b></p>
     <h1><b><u>COURSES AND DURATION</u></b></h1>
     <p>
         After completing class 12th, any student can do the followig courses in music:
         <ul><b>
             <li>Three courses are available on Bachelor's level - B.A.(Music), B.F.A.(Music), B.P.A(Music).<br>
            These degree courses ae of <b>three years</b> duration conducted by various art schools in India.</li><br>
            <li>There are also Diploma and Certificate courses in Music.</li><br>
            <li>Master's in Music, M.Phil(Music), Ph.D. in Music are some of the higher degree courses.</li>
        </b></ul>
        </p>
       <p> A student can select Vocal or Instruments for his/her music training depending upon his/her own interest.
     </p>

              <li>Composer</li>
              <li>Music director</li>
              <li>Producer</li>
              <li>Music editor</li>
              <li>Music supervisor</li>
              <li>Music publisher</li>
              <li>Song writer</li>
              <li>Recording engineer</li>
              <li>Instrumentalist</li>
              <li>Studio manager</li>
          </b></ul> 
     </p>
